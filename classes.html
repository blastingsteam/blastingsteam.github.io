Registration Details & Activities

We offer hybrid workshops, thus meeting online or in-person during weekends. A total of 4 classes and two STEAM events are included in the registration fee. The classes are conducted by different STEAM Volunteers and coordinated by the Blasting STEAM Executive Volunteer Team.. We have different STEAM workshops for kids through which they learn leadership and teamwork skill sets. The workshop focuses mainly on activities relating to science, technology, engineering, art, and math. Students learn to try new challenges and experiences that inspire and support them, while interacting with individuals with similar ideologies and values. Tuition for classes is $175. Space is limited. Payment must be completed in order to secure your spot. Registration will be based on a first-come-first serve basis. NO MAKEUPS/REFUNDS. All parents are required to attend a mandatory parent meeting on the first day of class. Dates will be announced after registration.
