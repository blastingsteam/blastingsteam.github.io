Our Mission & Vision

Through S.T.E.A.M (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, and Math logic) activities, we stay connected, create the next generation of innovators, and encourage our members to try new challenges and experiences that inspire and support them, paving the way for new paths in our community. We also try new challenges and experiences that inspire and support our members, paving the way for new paths in our community.

Past Activities

As a club we participate in many workshops such as science experiments, technology/coding, engineering water robots, robotics, glow art, STEAM art, businesses, finance, team building, challenging events, and much more. The classes are for students ages 7-13 years. We also have 13+ Youth volunteering opportunities available.
