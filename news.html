🏆 JCSC BSC Debate Tournament — 3rd Place!

Huge congratulations to the JCSC Blasting STEAM Club! After six months of dedicated preparation, our teams made a spectacular debut at their first-ever County Level Debate Tournament. We are incredibly proud to announce that the JCSC BSC Team—comprising Aarya Jain, Aadeen Shah, and Shaswath Shah—secured 3rd Place in the Novice Round, finishing the competition with a flawless 4-0 record! 🏆🚀 Our other talented participants who showed incredible skill and determination were Jayen Shah, Panav Shah, Paarth Jain, Ridhaan Ranka, Kayan Paliwal, Prisha Shah, Ahaana Shah, Miraya Mehta, Charvi Vakharia, Reva Barjatiya & Anushka Shah. Most of our teams successfully won two rounds, while all members embraced the challenge of competing for the very first time. We are so proud of everyone's hard work and growth. This program was expertly led by National Debate Champion and Youth Volunteer Arham, with vital coaching and support from Shray P., Arav D., and Shrey M. A special thank you goes out to our STEAM Team, our dedicated Youth Volunteers, and our Parents. This achievement was made possible through the full support of the JCSC. — Rupal Shah, Blasting STEAM Club

Walking Robotics

On February 26, 2023, a group of elementary schoolers participated in an exciting workshop all about walking robots. The workshop was designed to teach the children about the mechanics of robots and how they move. The students were tasked with building their own walking robots using materials such as motors and batteries. They were guided through the process by knowledgeable instructors who provided step-by-step instructions and answered any questions the children had.

Vedic Math

Vedic math is a highly efficient method of numerical computation that enables rapid problem-solving in arithmetic, algebra, geometry, calculus, and more. This class offers a unique opportunity to learn this ancient system of mathematics and gain new insights into the fascinating world of number theory.
