No Plastic Spoons

The Blasting STEAM Club humbly requests all Jain Center members to kindly bring metal spoons to all future Pathshala events. We urge you to support this initiative by spreading the word within your respective groups and encouraging parents and children to bring their own metal spoons. Your contribution towards this cause will undoubtedly set us on a path towards progress and positive change. We extend our heartfelt gratitude to you for your cooperation and ask that you kindly remember to pack your family's metal spoons before leaving for the event. Thank you in advance for your participation.

Walking Robotics

On February 26, 2023, a group of elementary schoolers participated in an exciting workshop all about walking robots. The workshop was designed to teach the children about the mechanics of robots and how they move. The students were tasked with building their own walking robots using materials such as motors and batteries. They were guided through the process by knowledgeable instructors who provided step-by-step instructions and answered any questions the children had.

Python Classes

For the 2022-2023 year, we have decided to include Python classes for ages 9 and up. Students will learn data types, strings, booleans, integers, variables, input/output, arithmetical operations, complex functions, loops, conditionals, and how to integrate libraries.
